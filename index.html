Executive Summary

Ability Inclusion Alliance is a women-led organization advancing full inclusion for persons with all disabilities, emphasizing neurodivergent conditions that are routinely overlooked.

Our mission is to shift attitudes and build the digital infrastructure that closes the gap between Africa's disability population and meaningful educational opportunity. We are psychologists, specialists, and advocates with lived experience.

The Invisible Disability Crisis

Neurodivergent conditions—autism, ADHD, dyslexia—face a compounded layer of invisibility in Kenya. Children are often labelled as "stubborn" rather than supported.

"Kenya Sign Language (KSL) is almost entirely absent from digital learning platforms, leaving Deaf learners fundamentally excluded."

Our Target Impact Areas

  • KSL-interpreted video content
  • Neurodivergent-friendly UX/UI
  • Caregiver psychosocial support

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Assessment (Mo 1-3)

Individual learner screening and digital tool accessibility testing with initial user cohorts to establish baselines.

Phase 2: Implementation (Mo 4-9)

Weekly digital learning sessions, localized content deployment, and monthly caregiver support group meetings.

Phase 3: Evaluation (Mo 10-12)

Comprehensive impact assessment and packaging of the documentation for continental replication and scaling.
