Executive Summary

Ability Inclusion Alliance is a women-led organization advancing full inclusion for persons with all disabilities, emphasizing neurodivergent conditions.

Our mission is to shift attitudes and build the digital infrastructure that closes the gap between Africa's disability population and meaningful educational opportunity.

Multidisciplinary Team (Psychologists & Educators)
Focus on Invisible Disabilities

"Inclusion is not a dream, it's our mission."

The Gap

80%+ unemployment for persons with disabilities in Kenya and minimal EdTech accessibility.

The Solution

Disability-first digital tools, KSL-integrated video content, and neurodivergent-friendly UI.

Impact

Direct support for 300+ learners and 200+ caregivers in the initial Umoja pilot site.
